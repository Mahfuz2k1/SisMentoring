What is One2One Mentoring?

Our One2One Mentoring is a personal, confidential guidance program for young women and mothers.
Here, you can share your challenges, goals, and dreams with a mentor who truly listens.

How we do this?
We connect with you privately via video or WhatsApp call.
We help you identify your strengths, plan solutions, and stay motivated.
Everything is supportive, judgement-free, and based on Islamic values.
